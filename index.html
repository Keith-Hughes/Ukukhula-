Calling all esteemed educational institutions actively engaged in fostering academic excellence! BBD’s Ukukhula Educational Funding Programme presents a unique opportunity for educational establishments to join hands and secure financial support for their deserving students. This initiative is crafted to empower institutions in providing quality education by availing the necessary resources.

The role

We are an international software solutions company that solves real-world problems with innovative solutions and modern technology stacks. Our experience spans the education, financial services, gaming, insurance, telecoms and public sectors. We maintain our track record by using our vast business domain knowledge and world-class skills to successfully deliver digital solutions into client organisations.

What we’re looking for

If your institution is passionate about delivering quality education and is seeking financial support to enhance the learning experience for your students, then the BBD Educational Funding Programme is tailored for you!

If this is you, then our Ukukhula Educational Funding Programme is for you!

Basic Requirements for Educational Institutions:

  • Active involvement in providing education.
  • Clear objectives on how funding will be utilized to enhance educational offerings
  • A commitment to maintaining academic standards and fostering student success.

When applying, don’t forget to include:

  • Comprehensive proposal outlining the objectives, initiatives, and expected outcomes of the funding.
  • Institutional profile highlighting achievements, programs, and areas of impact
  • Financial breakdown detailing how the funds will be allocated and utilized

Please note

By expressing interest in this funding opportunity, you acknowledge and agree that BBD may conduct a thorough review of the institution's background, goals, and financial transparency.

If your educational institution is eager to join hands with BBD and secure funding to further the cause of quality education, we welcome your funding proposals. Connect with us to explore this collaborative opportunity in advancing educational excellence.
